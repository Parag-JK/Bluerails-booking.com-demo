When Do You Actually Get Paid?

According to Booking.com official documentation4, your payout timeline depends entirely on whether you process your own payments or let Booking.com handle them. If Booking.com handles the payment, you are typically paid one of three ways:

Daily Payouts

Processed one day after the guest checks out. Bank processing takes 3-5 additional business days.

Weekly Payouts

Processed every Thursday for guests who checked out the previous week.

Monthly Payouts

Processed by the 15th of the month for all check-outs in the previous month.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does Booking.com charge hotels?

Booking.com charges a commission typically between 10% and 25% per booking. For most independent hotels and short-term rentals, the standard average base commission is 15%1.

2. Does Booking.com charge commission on taxes and fees?

Generally, Booking.com does not charge commission on local city taxes or tourist taxes. However, standard fees that you add to the room rate—such as cleaning fees or pet fees—are subject to standard commission1.

3. Does Booking.com charge on cancellations?

If a guest cancels for free, you do not pay a commission. However, if you have a non-refundable policy and you charge the guest a cancellation fee, Booking.com will charge a commission on that retained amount1.

4. What is the Preferred Partner fee?

To boost visibility in search results, hotels can join the Preferred Partner program. This generally adds an extra 3% to your base commission, raising the average cost from 15% to 18%2.

5. Does Booking.com take payment?

Yes, you can opt into "Payments by Booking.com." If you use this service, Booking.com handles the credit card transaction, but charges an additional payment processing fee of 1.1% to 3.1% depending on the payment method3.

6. Can hotels negotiate Booking.com commission?

Small, independent hotels rarely have the leverage to negotiate baseline commissions. However, large hotel groups with massive inventory can sometimes negotiate better portfolio rates with their account managers.
